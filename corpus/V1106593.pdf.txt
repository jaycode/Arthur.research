Description
Charming character home at Granville & Connaught in prestigious Shaughnessy! Original 1953 but substantially renovated in 2011. Main floor features a gorgeous family, living & dining room, an updated granite & stainless  steel kitchen with eating area, plus a spacious recreation room with wet bar. Upper level features 5 bedrooms including an extra large master with spa-like ensuite.  Renovations include: windows, flooring, carpet, doors, lighting, interior & exterior paint throughout. Beautifully landcaped with outdoor pool & park-like private backyard.
