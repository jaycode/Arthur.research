With beauty and scenery so stunning & unique, the Sandstone estate has become a back drop in the next season of CBC's Heartland. Nestled atop a ridge in the foothills  only 30 min from Calgary, this elegant home with it's magnificent views from every room often feels as if it is floating alone in paradise. Come experience what this unique  home has to offer including a grand staircase, 10' ceilings, 2 offices, a library, 7 fireplaces, 8 balconies, 7 garages & car wash, 2-storey tropical atrium with hot tub &  fountain, gourmet kitchen, finished attic bedrooms, 4-storey elevator, and walkout lower level wine cellar & large rec room with bar and fireplace. The exterior features  copper eaves, cedar shingles, imported sandstone, reclaimed pine beams and an elegant windowed entrance. Gated security entrance. Located on a natural aquifer  system providing unlimited drinking water to 3 wells. Natural gas powered generator w/ capacity supply entire property. Additional features in the property brochure.
