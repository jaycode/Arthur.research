Description
This masterpiece in the rolling foothills near Priddis only 40 min from DT Calgary, has more than 10,000sqft of living space. 68 acres of mature landscaping divided by Fish  Creek provides a beautiful & tranquil setting. Built in Post & Beam style boasting vaulted ceilings w/ skylights letting in sunshine while you relax by any of the 6 stone  fireplaces. Rich woods,natural stone accents & sleek marble flow throughout. A gourmet kitchen showcases marble counters,state-of-the-art appliances & AGA oven,  handcrafted detailing & stunning copper finishes. Combining finer points of country living with modern luxury including a wine cellar, home gym,golf simulator,RO  treatment, culvert well system & water conditioners. For the equestrian lover, a 14,000sqft heated arena w/viewing suite, round pen, 20,000sqft outdoor riding arena &  auto water feeders. Heated Quonset & 6 car garage w/wash bay. Surrounded by riding trails & animals, the nature of the land makes it feel as if you're living in your own  provincial park.
