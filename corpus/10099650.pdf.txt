Hawk's View is arguably the best view property in Kelowna. Contemporary meets comfort in this iconic Charles Stinson inspired home on the Hart Road bench elevated  above the Harvest Golf Course. Day or night it can be compared to no other with expansive 180 degree views from Peachland to downtown Kelowna & Knox Mountain.  Finesse, sophistication & architectural characteristics are ever present. This home is built to entertain or simply to enjoy life. The 11 acre location is private & serene where  country living can be experienced at its very best, yet minutes from downtown. Ideally located to the Aberdeen Hall Preparatory School.
